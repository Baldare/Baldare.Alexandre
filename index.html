Alexandre Baldare

I am currently a high school teacher in mathematics at Cité scolaire Françoise Combes, Montpellier.

I did my Ph.D. in the Institut Montpéliairain Alexander Grothendieck, at the University of Montpellier under the supervision of Moulay Benameur.

My research interests are in Non Commutative Geometry, Operator algebras and index theory. The title of my Ph.D. thesis is Théorie de l'indice pour les familles d'opérateurs G-transversalement elliptiques.
